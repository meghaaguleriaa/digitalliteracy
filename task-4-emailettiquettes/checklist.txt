DO's 
1. Build a professional profile (clean bio, proper photo, academic interests)
2. Share learning content (projects, coding progress, tech events)
3. Verify sources before posting tech/news updates
4. Network with seniors, developers, and communities (LinkedIn, GitHub)
5. Maintain a positive digital footprint (future recruiters may see it)


DON'Ts
1. Post offensive memes, hate content, or controversial opinions publicly
2. Share college credentials, code, or private project data
3. Blindly follow or repost fake tech news or AI trends
4. Arguing aggressively in comment sections (especially on professional platforms)
5. Waste excessive time scrolling instead of learning
