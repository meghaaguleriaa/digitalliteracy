Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
2. Do not click on unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
3. Always verify the identity of callers before trusting any financial request.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
6. While using UPI, remember: you don’t need to enter PIN to receive money—only to send it.
7. Carefully check UPI payment requests and QR codes before approving or scanning.
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions.
9. Regularly update your apps and phone software to stay protected from security threats.
10. Install apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or App Store.
